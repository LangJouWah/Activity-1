Le Sserafim

a South Korean girl group formed by Source Music. The group consists of five members: Sakura, Kim Chae-won, Huh Yun-jin, Kazuha, and Hong Eun-chae. Originally a six-member group, Kim Ga-ram left on July 20, 2022, after the termination of her exclusive contract.
Le Sserafim is an anagram of the phrase "I'm Fearless." The phrase represents how the group is "determined to move forward with unswerving fearlessness in the eyes of the world."
